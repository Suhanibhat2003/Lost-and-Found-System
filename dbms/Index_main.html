Lost & Found

The Lost and Found System is a digital platform designed to streamline the process of reporting and retrieving lost items, improving the efficiency of item recovery in various settings, such as restaurants, public transportation, educational institutions, garden, and large events.

LOST

Informing the relevant authorities or organizations about an item that you have misplaced or lost.

FOUND

Informing the appropriate authorities or taking necessary steps to notify others about an item that you have found but do not own.

CLAIM

Refers to the process where a person asserts ownership or the right to possess an item that has been lost or found.
